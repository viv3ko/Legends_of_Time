Owners Bio:

DAN DeCLARK OPENED LEGENDS OF TIME ON AUG. 1, 1993 IN MILFORD MICHIGAN. SINCE THEN, HE HAS OPENED ANOTHER STORE IN OWOSSO, MICHIGAN AND NOW HAS A PARTNER, ROBERT WAINIO.

IN JUNE 2003 WE CLOSED THE OWOSSO STORE, AND NOW HAVE A STORE IN DOWNTOWN ROCHESTER, MICHIGAN. OUR MILFORD STORE ON AUG. 1, 2017 WILL HAVE BEEN FOR 25 YEARS AND THE ROCHESTER STORE ON JUNE 1, 2017 WILL HAVE BEEN OPEN FOR 16 YEARS.

WE ARE ALWAYS TRYING AND ACHIEVING OUR EXPECTATIONS OF WHAT WE WANT AS BUSINESS OWNERS.

BOTH OF US HAVING NATIVE ANCESTRY, WE WANT TO HONOR THE NATIVE AMERICAN INDIAN CULTURE BY TEACHING THE COMMUNITY ON THE HISTORY, MEANING AND DEDICATION OF THE NATIVE AMERICAN PEOPLE. AS BUSINESS OWNERS, WE TAKE GREAT PRIDE IN SERVICING YOU, OUR CUSTOMERS. WE FEEL OUR MERCHANDISE IS MADE WITH GREAT PRIDE AND EXCELLENT QUALITY. WE HAVE THE LARGEST SELECTIONS IN THE MICHIGAN AREA.
